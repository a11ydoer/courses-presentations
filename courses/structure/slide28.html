HTML5 Headings Outline Example

Example from HTML5 specification

HTML5 Outline Algorithm

  1. Apples (H1 in accessibility API)
    1. Taste (H2 in accessibility API)
      1. Sweet (H3 in accessibility API)
    2. Color (H2 in accessibility API)
<body>
 <h1>Apples</h1>
 <p>Apples are fruit.</p>
 <section>
  <h1>Taste</h1>
  <p>They taste lovely.</p>
  <section>
   <h1>Sweet</h1>
   <p>Red apples are sweeter than green ones.</p>
  </section>
 </section>
 <section>
  <h1>Color</h1>
  <p>Apples come in various colors.</p>
 </section>
</body>

same as
