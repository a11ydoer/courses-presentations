Overall Implementation Scores for Websites in 2015 and 2017

Description of Graph Features
  • The Figure shows two sets of data points, one set for 2015 and the other for 2017.
  • Between 2015 and 2017, the figure shows a slight improvement of in overall implementation scores of about 2.2%.
  • The figure has two strong features.
    1. The first 100 websites generally follow a linear progression from an implementation score of 20 to 60.
    2. The next 500 websites show an approximately linear progression of implementation score from 60 to 90.
    3. There are a few implementations scores near 100 (which would be considered an ideal score).
